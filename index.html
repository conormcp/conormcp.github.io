Conor McPartland

Postdoctor researcher in wide area astrophysical surveys

Cosmic Dawn Center, Neils Bohr Institute, Copenhagen, Denmark

I am an observational astronomer working on deep wide area surveys with the Euclid Space Telescope and Subaru Hyper SuprimeCam. My main interests lie in galaxy evolution with a specific focus on the role of galaxy morphology and AGN activity on star-formation in galaxies.
